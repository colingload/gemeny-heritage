## Migration Notes

- All assets should be downloaded from gemeny.com and placed in the appropriate `assets/` subdirectory
- Photos should be preserved at original resolution — do not resize or compress
- Audio files are already MP3 — preserve as-is
- The War.pdf should be preserved as-is AND transcribed into markdown
- Tin types and old photographs may benefit from enhanced scans if originals are accessible
- The bad_packaging.htm page (documenting the damaged Tandberg deck shipment) is a fun artifact but low priority
