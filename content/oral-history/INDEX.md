# Andrew Gemeny Oral History Recordings

A series of audio recordings made between 1974 and 1980 by Andrew Gemeny of Brandywine, Maryland. Andrew (known as "Pop" to his grandsons) recorded these stories on cassette tape, encouraged by his grandson Steven and his brother.

Andrew himself noted the lack of order and structure in his recordings, and suggested that someone might one day find a way to rearrange them into a better sequence.

## Digitization Notes

Steven Gemeny digitized the tapes in 2007-2008 after locating a Tandberg 330 3-motor cassette deck on eBay. Processing pipeline:
1. Recorded cassettes to WAV files using GoldWave software
2. Filtered to reduce extraneous noise
3. Compressed to level speaking volume variations
4. Applied pop and click filter for microphone handling noise
5. Merged left/right tracks (mono to stereo)
6. Exported as MP3
